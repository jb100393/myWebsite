Newcastle United




Everything you need to know about Newcastle. Newcastle upon Tyne is home to the famous football club, Newcastle United, with more than 4.3 million fans around the world. Some of the most iconic landmarks in Newcastle include the Angel of the North, St James' Park, and the River Tyne. The River Tyne stretches for up to 118 km, while St James' Park has a seating capacity of 60,000 people.
